# 3D Print Business Simulator

**Live Demo:** https://husamr-tgdar.github.io/3d-print-simulator/

A comprehensive financial simulation tool for planning and modeling a 3D printing business in Bahrain. Built with a professional dark manufacturing theme inspired by industrial control systems.

## Features

- **24-month financial projections** with detailed month-by-month breakdowns
- **4 growth scenarios**: Pessimistic, Realistic, B2B Focus, and Optimistic
- **Interactive dashboard** with real-time metric cards and charts
- **Full cost modeling**: COGS, OpEx, equipment triggers, cash flow
- **Capacity planning**: Print hours tracking and equipment scaling
- **Dark manufacturing theme** with electric cyan accents and smooth animations

## Business Model

- **Location**: Bahrain
- **Currency**: BD (Bahraini Dinar)
- **Products**: Small, medium, and large 3D prints, plus bulk orders
- **Starting Capital**: BD 8,355 (after setup costs)

## Technologies

- Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript (single-file application)
- Chart.js for data visualization
- Custom fonts: Orbitron, Manrope, JetBrains Mono
- Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile

## Usage

Simply open the link above or download \`index.html\` and open it in any modern browser. No installation or dependencies required.

## Customization

All business parameters are configurable through the sidebar:
- Growth rates and starting conditions
- Product mix and pricing
- Print times and costs
- Equipment purchase triggers
- Operating expenses
